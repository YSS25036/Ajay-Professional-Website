A Harvard Business Review study finds that certain patterns of AI use drive cognitive fatigue, while others can help reduce burnout. The piece references Gas Town—an open-source platform for orchestrating swarms of Claude Code agents—and the "palpable sense of stress" some users feel when AI moves faster than comprehension. Important for leaders thinking about sustainable AI adoption and team well-being.
