![](images/chris-sig-200x200.jpeg)  I was six months into the seat as the new CEO, coming onboard as part of a two-company merger between two of the top three software providers in a specialty healthcare market.  The opportunity in front of the company was very exciting but the work to integrate our businesses and unify our approach was daunting – especially on the product engineering and technology side of our business.  As a non-technical CEO, I needed a lot of help here.

From the first conversation I had with Ajay, it was apparent that he possessed a unique combination of deep, technical knowledge and skills, experience in high growth, dynamic environments coupled with a very high emotional IQ.  Ajay quickly learned the background and context of our particular situation, built strong communication channels and relationships with key stakeholders, and began to educate myself and the organization on how to organize our integration ideas into a cohesive and actionable plan.

Throughout the process, communication was stellar. Ajay kept us informed at every step, ensuring alignment and transparency across all stakeholders. Their proactive approach to problem-solving anticipated hurdles before they arose, keeping our integration timeline on track and minimizing disruptions to our operations.

One of the most impressive aspects of working with Ajay was his ability to adapt and pivot in real-time. As unforeseen circumstances emerged, they remained agile, adjusting strategies and implementing solutions with precision and efficiency.

Thanks to Ajay’s invaluable guidance and support, our M&A integration was not only successful but exceeded our expectations. Our technology organization is now stronger, more cohesive, and better positioned to drive our business forward.
